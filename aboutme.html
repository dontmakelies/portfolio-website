Hello, nice to meet you!

My name is Madison Mercado. I am an experienced journalist and budding graphic designer.

I will be graduating from Boston University in May 2024 with a Bachelor's degree in Journalism with a minor in International Relations. During college, I've written dozens of stories about the city of Boston through a full-time metro news co-op at the Boston Globe, a news internship at NPR-affiliate station GBH, my coursework, and a work-study job in the Marketing and Communications Center at BU's College of Communications.

When I'm not writing, you can find me learning new techniques to grow my graphic design know-how. Posting my work to Instagram has brought me a lot of joy over the years. I've made posters of my inner thoughts, sticker designs based on my favorite albums, brand concepts, and more in between.
