Hello, nice to meet you!

My name's Madison Mercado. I am a copywriter by day, designer and illustrator by night, and a journalist in a past life. But at my core, I am a creative.

I graduated from Boston University in May 2024 with a degree in journalism and a minor in international relations. I have worked at Walker Sands, a B2B marketing agency; the Marketing and Communications Center at BU's College of Communications; the Boston Globe, New England's largest newspaper; and NPR-affiliate GBH Radio.

I am based in Boston, Massachusetts, but I'm originally from Washington state and love to go home for the beautiful PNW views. Don't ask me about stationery and crafts, LEGOs, matcha, or Marvel movies if you don't want to get me started ;) (but please do because I love to talk about it).
