Newswriting in Boston

I have covered city news for The Boston Globe, GBH Radio, and in my journalism classes at BU. Below is a selection of the work I’ve done for each publication.

Reporting for GBH

As a news and radio intern, I wrote cut scripts for top-of-the-hour newscasts for GBH 89.7 FM. I covered Boston city news, including the Massachusetts Gaming Commission and local protests.
